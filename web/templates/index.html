Car Parts Detection

How to use:
Select your car image and the confidence threshold and press Predict. The model will analyze the image and display the detected parts.

Results:
Image of the car with bounding boxes and segmentation masks and a results table are displayed. Confidence indicates certainty, box is the detected area, and mask is the segmentation outline.
